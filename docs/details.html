About

Marina Square - Uptown Colombo is Sri Lanka’s first and largest harbor front luxury condominium project developed by three internationally reputed industry giants; Access Engineering PLC, China Harbour Engineering Company and Mustafa Singapore. The complex will be developed on an expansive five-acre land consisting of 1068 condos over 36 floors across 5 towers as well as having two acres exclusively allocated for recreational facilities and common areas. Located minutes away from the anticipated developments such as the Colombo International Financial City (Port City) and Elevated Highway, Marina Square – Uptown Colombo offers modern living spaces with infinite ROI possibilities and unmatched rental opportunities.
