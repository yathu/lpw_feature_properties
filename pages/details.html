About

Marina Square - Uptown Colombo is Sri Lanka’s first and largest harbor front luxury condominium project developed by three internationally reputed industry giants; Access Engineering PLC, China Harbour Engineering Company and Mustafa Singapore. The complex will be developed on an expansive five-acre land consisting of 1068 condos over 36 floors across 5 towers as well as having two acres exclusively allocated for recreational facilities and common areas. Located minutes away from the anticipated developments such as the Colombo International Financial City (Port City) and Elevated Highway, Marina Square – Uptown Colombo offers modern living spaces with infinite ROI possibilities and unmatched rental opportunities.

Features

Club House & Function Halls

Entertaining guests and finding a space to wind-down in privacy is made so much more convenient with a common bar and lounge space for residents of Marina Square - Uptown Colombo. A unique and luxurious atmosphere has been created for patrons to enjoy their free time from the foot of their doorsteps.

Infinity Pool and Kids Pool

An infinity pool set amidst the backdrop of the Colombo harbour will provide a captivating view of one of the main arteries of the city. Kicking back after a long day or even relaxing will be a blissful and care-free experience through the ever-changing and vibrant skyline of Colombo.

Children's Play Area

One of the largest kid’s play areas in luxury urban development, Marina Square Uptown Colombo is a wonderland for kids and adults alike. Teach your kids to ride a bike or camp out under the stars, all within the sanctuary of Marina Square – Uptown Colombo

Lifestyle & Entertainment

The urban luxury lifestyle at Marina Square Uptown Colombo offers a range of entertainment for kids and adults alike. Over 2 acres of landscaped gardens and sky decks create a multitude of entertainment locations and casual hangouts to entertain and enthrall your family friends.
